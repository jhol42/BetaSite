History

Team 60 was founded at Kingman High School in 1996 by an engineer at Laron Inc. Team 60's first season in "First Robotics Competition" (FRC) was 1997 competing in the "Toroid Terror" challenge.

Eventually, the team expanded to include Kingman Academy of Learning High School, in addition to Kingman High School. As Kingman grew, the team also welcomed students from Lee Williams High School as well as homeschool students. Now the team also includes students in 7th grade and up.

Recently in 2022, Team 60 celebrated their 25th season competing in the FRC competition.

Building Funds

Team 60 has started a project to build a community robotics center on Kingman High School's campus. This building will provide a space for our team to work on, machine, program, and test robots. It will also provide a space that other community robotics programs can use. Currently the project has been started, but we are still raising additional funding.

Please consider supporting us! Donations can be made on our GoFundMe, directly made to our account at Foothills bank, or sent to Kingman Robotics at PO Box 6492 Kingman, AZ 86402. For more information, please contact us at firstteam60robotics@gmail.com
